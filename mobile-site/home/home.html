Ultimate Summer Party 2018

Are you ready for USP part 2? Ultimate Summer Party™ 2018 promises to rival last year with the same great games, good music, beautiful weather and indomitable heroes. We are expecting an even bigger turnout than last year with travelers coming from any where including both American coasts. To accomodate, we are planning on bringing even more to USP2018. Let us know what you want in your USP experience and we'll make it happen. And as always, cowards are not welcome. Watch USP2017






There will be multiple open grills for a BYOM (bring your own meat) cookout. The venue includes a 300 foot private pond open for unlimited swimming, fishing, and canoeing. The event concludes with a bonfire-lit closing ceremony for those true heroes who conquer the day's events
