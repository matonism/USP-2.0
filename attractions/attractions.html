Dizzy Bat Relay
This game is played in teams of 4. The first player must finish a drink and immediately place their forehead to the bat's handle while placing the other end of the bat firmly on the ground. The player must spin around the bat 10 times, always maintaining contact between the bat and the ground and their forehead. After 10 full revolutions, the player must hit a can (which will be thrown by an unbiased pitcher). Once contact is made, the next player goes. The team who has every player hit the can first, wins the game.


Slip-n-Slide Flip Cup
This game is played in teams of 4. The teams line up at the end of a slip n slide. Each team chooses a player to go first and that player must run down the slide to reach a table at the end. Once they reach the table, they must drink from one of four cups placed on their side. After finishing the drink, they must flip the cup by regular flip cup standards. Once they achieve a successful flip, the next player may run down the slide and flip their cup. The first team to successfully flip all their cups wins.


Garbage Can Pong
Garbage Can pong is played with 2 teams of 2. The rules are similar to table pong, but on a much larger scale. The cups are replaced with garbage cans and the ping pong ball will be replaced with a volleyball. Other than that, its beer pong. Make all ten cans, drink when a can is made, Islands, re-rack, rebuttal, etc... The first team to make all ten cans wins


Beersbee
This game is played by teams of 2. Two poles will be placed approximately 36 feet apart. Each pole will have an empty bottle on top of it. Each team stands behind their respective pole. Teams then take turns throwing a frisbee at the pole to score points. A team who hits the pole gains 1 point. A team who knocks off the beer bottle without the opposing team catching it earns 2 additional points. The first team to 15 points (win by 2) wins. Each player must drink each time the opponent scores a point.
